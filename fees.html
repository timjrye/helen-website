Availability & Fees

When you make contact with me by email or telephone we will agree to an initial meeting. This will be an opportunity for you to talk about your issues and to find out if we feel we can work together. I will also carry out a professional assessment of your situation and the suitability of counselling for you. I will let you know if I do not think that counselling is your best option and we can talk about what else may be appropriate for you. I charge a fixed fee of £45 for an initial assessment meeting.

If we decide to proceed we will agree a time when we can meet each week. We will also agree an appropriate fee, which will range from £35 to £80 per session dependant on your financial situation.

I also offer reduced fees for those in training.
